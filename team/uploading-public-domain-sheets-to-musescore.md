### 2. Upload to Musescore

Choose **Unlisted**

![](https://i.gyazo.com/d0c724e416fbeec190ae16050e12468b.gif)



Please fill in the **Score Details:**

* [ ] **Ensemble:** Solo
* [ ] **Genre:** Classical
* [ ] **Title:** Czerny Op. 599 - No. \[\#\#\] \[Title\]
* [ ] **Description:** \[blank\]
* [ ] **Tags:** Czerny
